About Me

I work as a software engineer at Grammarly in New York. I graduated from Duke University in 2024, where I studied Computer Science and English.

During college, I served as a teaching assistant and participated in programs focused on increasing diversity in tech. I also wrote for The Duke Chronicle and The Trek, and was involved with the Rotaract Club.

I'm interested in the intersection of technology and communication, and enjoy working on projects that help people express themselves more clearly.
